# Execution Playbooks

## The 5-Phase Launch Playbook

Every autonomous business follows the same fundamental launch sequence. This playbook applies regardless of category.

### Phase 1: Validate (Week 1)
- Identify the niche and target audience with specificity — "freelance designers who need invoice templates" not "designers"
- Research competition using Product Hunt, Empire Flippers, and direct search
- Validate demand: check search volume, subreddit activity, and existing solution pricing
- Define the minimum viable product (MVP) — the smallest thing you can sell
- **Gate:** Do not proceed unless you find evidence of paying customers in the niche

### Phase 2: Build MVP (Week 2-3)
- Generate the PRP using the appropriate category template (SaaS, digital product, etc.)
- Execute the PRP against the codebase — AI builds the initial version
- Run all validation gates: syntax, tests, integration, deployment
- Set up Stripe for payments with test mode verification
- **Gate:** MVP must handle the core use case end-to-end with zero manual steps

### Phase 3: Launch & Monitor (Week 4)
- Deploy to production on Vercel with monitoring enabled
- Activate the governance framework — set approval gates and audit logging
- Launch to initial audience: Product Hunt, relevant communities, direct outreach
- Monitor all metrics: uptime, errors, conversion, revenue
- **Gate:** First paying customer acquired, system runs 72 hours without critical errors

### Phase 4: Automate (Week 5-8)
- Identify manual bottlenecks from the monitoring data
- Automate customer support with AI chatbot trained on common queries
- Set up automated marketing: email sequences, content publishing, social media
- Implement feedback loops — product improvements based on usage data
- **Gate:** Business runs for one full week with less than 1 hour of human intervention

### Phase 5: Scale & Diversify (Ongoing)
- Optimize conversion funnel using A/B testing and analytics
- Expand product line or feature set based on customer demand
- Launch complementary businesses in adjacent categories
- Reinvest 20% of revenue into new autonomous business experiments
- **Gate:** Monthly revenue exceeds operational costs by 3x

## Key Principles

- **Speed over perfection:** Launch in weeks, not months. Iterate based on real data.
- **Validate before building:** Never spend more than a week on an unvalidated idea.
- **Automate incrementally:** Do not try to automate everything on day one. Use Progressive Autonomy.
- **Measure everything:** If you cannot measure it, you cannot automate it.
- **Fail fast, pivot faster:** Kill businesses that do not show traction within 30 days and reallocate resources.
