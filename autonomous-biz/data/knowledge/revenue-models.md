# Revenue Models & Pricing Strategies

## Core Revenue Models

### Subscription (MRR)
Predictable monthly recurring revenue. Best for SaaS and content platforms. Typical tiers: Free (lead generation), Starter ($9-29/mo), Pro ($49-99/mo), Enterprise (custom). Optimize for annual plans to reduce churn and improve cash flow.

### One-Time Sales
Single purchase for digital products, templates, and courses. Price based on value delivered, not cost to produce. AI-generated products can be priced at $5-$500 depending on niche and perceived value. Bundle products to increase average order value.

### Usage-Based
Charge per API call, per generation, or per transaction. Best for AI-as-a-Service. Aligns cost with value — customers pay more as they get more value. Include a base fee to ensure minimum revenue per customer.

### Commission / Take Rate
Marketplace model — take a percentage of each transaction. Typical rates: 5-20% depending on vertical. Keep rates low initially to attract supply, increase gradually as the platform becomes essential.

### Advertising & Affiliate
Content monetization through ads (CPM/CPC) and affiliate commissions (5-50% per sale). Requires significant traffic volume. Best as supplementary revenue alongside subscriptions or products.

## Pricing Strategy Principles

- **Value-based pricing:** Price based on the outcome delivered, not the cost of AI compute
- **Anchor high:** Start with a higher price and offer discounts rather than starting low
- **Test relentlessly:** A/B test pricing pages — small changes can double conversion
- **Segment by willingness to pay:** Offer tiers that let customers self-select into the right price point
- **Annual discounts:** Offer 20% off for annual billing to reduce churn and improve lifetime value
